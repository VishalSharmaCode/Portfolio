    Introduction

    Everything in the farm is mostly dependent on humans. According to edurev around 45% of the world's population is enggaged in agriculture. When the crops are supplied with adequate water the farmer has to switch off the moter manually.
    And when he has to irrigate his other fields which are away then he has to physically swap the water with pipes from one field to another .
    However , agronic can be proven a wise idea in these conditions.
    A farmer can control all these with the help of agronic. The farmer can swap the direction of water from one field with the help of application - Agronic.

    Features

    Agronic have many features which help farmers in their hard working life. Here are some features are following:

  • Soil Moisture: Using Agronic farmers can monitor moisture level of the field on mobile application.
  • Water monitoring: Farmers can monitor water level of the field using the mobile application and a single sensor.
  • Pump Control: Agronic help farmers to control their water pump using mobile application.
  • Water Direction Control: Using mobile application farmers can control the direction of water valvs.
